June Jamboree 2023

Published August 06, 2025

Get ready for a nostalgic trip down memory lane with the upcoming "June Jamboree," a fund-raising event promising an evening filled with fun, music, and community spirit. Scheduled for June 30th at 5:00 PM, this event offers a fantastic opportunity to enjoy a lively atmosphere while contributing to a worthy cause. The retro-themed promotional material, featuring a classic cassette tape, hints at a celebration that will evoke fond memories and create new ones.

The June Jamboree is designed to be an engaging experience for all attendees. The promise of "Fun Games, Music, and Encouragement!" suggests a diverse lineup of activities catering to various interests.

Imagine participating in lighthearted games, enjoying a selection of music that might span different eras, and being surrounded by an uplifting and positive environment. This type of event fosters a sense of camaraderie and provides a relaxed setting to connect with others in the community. The emphasis on "encouragement" further implies that the Jamboree aims to be a source of positivity and inspiration for everyone who participates.

This special gathering is also a fund-raising event, adding a meaningful layer to the enjoyment. By attending the June Jamboree, individuals will not only have a memorable time but will also be contributing to an important initiative. The ticket price of PHP 150, which includes food, makes it an accessible and value-for-money way to support this cause while indulging in an evening of entertainment and fellowship. The inclusion of food ensures that attendees can enjoy refreshments while participating in the various activities, making it a complete and enjoyable experience.

Events like the June Jamboree play a crucial role in building strong and supportive communities. They provide a platform for people to come together, socialize, and work towards a common goal.
